Integrated Technology Consultants (ITC) is an Indiana-based consultancy that equips trades, property professionals, and homeowners with technology that delivers peace of mind.

Our work blends technological expertise with a people-first mindset to deliver solutions that are practical, scalable, and impactful.

Our Consulting Services

Tech-First Consulting

We help organizations take a tech-first approach to rethink how support is delivered. Our role is to bridge the gap between available technologies and the people, workflows, and care models they are meant to serve.

Our services include:

  • Tech-readiness assessments for staff, spaces, and systems
  • Development of individualized and organization-wide technology plans
  • Smart home system planning, deployment, and education
  • Remote support readiness: infrastructure, workflow, and protocol planning
  • Guidance on integrating enabling technologies that promote autonomy, safety, and communication
  • Strategic consultation to support the shift from traditional models to tech-enabled systems of care
  • Note: We are consultants and implementation partners. We do not provide direct remote monitoring or caregiving, but we prepare your organization to confidently deliver, or partner to deliver, those services with the right technology in place.
